Privacy Policy

Your information

Registration information. When you sign up for MySQUAR applications, you will be required to provide us some personal information such as your name and email address. Once you register, you can go to your profile at any time to add more information about yourself, such as your date of birth, phone number, avatar. Your avatar and your MySQUAR name are publicly displayed.

Information you choose to share. The information we collect also includes information you chose to share when using our products, such as when you create a post, share a post, reply to a post, upload a photo or play a game. Status, gender, location, friend list, and age are also collected.

Information publicly available. Other users may view personal information such as username, avatar image, status, gender, location, friend list, and age are also shared publicly.

Activity information. We collect information whenever you have any interaction with MySQUAR applications. This may include time and date, type of activity, and technical information about the device, operating system and browser you use.

Information sharing and disclosure

Sharing information. MySQUAR does not rent, sell or share personal information with other people or third parties without your permission.

Third parties. When we have your permission, we may provide the information to trusted partners who work with MySQUAR under confidentiality agreements. These companies may use your personal information to assist MySQUAR to help improve or promote our services . However, these companies do not have any independent right to share these information.

Law and Harm. We believe that it is necessary to share your information if there is any request from legal authorities to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of MySQUAR’s terms of use.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to provide our services and features to you, to measure and improve those services and features, and to provide you with customer support.

We use the information we collect to prevent potentially illegal activities and terminate permanently of some function.

We use the information we collect to contact you with service-related announcements or send you our update notifications from time to time.

We use the information we collect to memorize your account. We may close your account if we receive a formal request from you or other proper legal request to do so.

Viewing, Changing or Removing Information. You can view, edit the information in your profile at any time by updating your profile information. The update takes effect immediately. You can delete your account by requesting it to our customer support.

Limitations on removal. When you delete your account or remove some information from your profile, copies of those information on other users’ profiles may remain if those information have been posted or shared with others.

Changes to this policy

MySQUAR may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you about changes by sending a notice to the email you registered with us.
