about us.

MySQUAR was started in June 2013, having identified a unique opportunity to
launch social media applications in the rapidly emerging Myanmar market. What
began as a pioneering project to launch an open source social app evolved into
our flagship product MyCHAT which launched in October 2014.

|

The MySQUAR team is a group of dynamic, international and tech-savy designers, developers and
engineers. The Company is managed by a tech and financial-driven team with extensive experience in
consumer technology development, operations management and finance. Executive management has
been living and doing business in Asia for many years.

what we do.

MySQUAR is a leading consumer technology company
created to enrich the lives of people through a broad,
accessible and authentic online experience.

We are the first social media provider focused solely on Myanmar
incorporating local culture and language in all that we do. MySQUAR has
established a strong social media platform targeting mobile phone users in
Myanmar with a large user base of over 7.5 million registered users as of
January 2017
