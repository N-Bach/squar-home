about us.

MySQUAR is the first media and entertainment company focused solely on Myanmar incorporating local culture and language in all that we do. MySQUAR has established a diverse platform targeting mobile phone users in Myanmar with a large user base of over 7.5 million registered users as of January 2017.

|

The MySQUAR team is a group of dynamic, international and tech-savvy designers, developers and engineers. The Company is managed by a tech and financial-driven team with extensive experience in consumer technology development, operations management and finance. Executive management has been living and doing business in Asia for many years.
